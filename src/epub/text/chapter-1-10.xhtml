Tzŭ-chʽin59 asked Tzŭ-kung,60 saying, "When our master61 comes to any country,62 he does not fail63 to learn all about its government. Does he ask his information? or is it given to him?"64

Tzŭ-kung said, "Our master is benign, upright, courteous, temperate, and complaisant, and thus he gets his information. The master's mode of asking information!—is it not different from that of other men?"65
