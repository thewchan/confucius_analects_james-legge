II 717

The Master said, "Of those who were with me in Chʽên and Tsʽai, there are none to be found to enter my door."718

Distinguished for their virtuous principles and practice, there were Yen Yuän, Min Tzǔ-chʽien, Jan Po-niu, and Chung-kung; for their ability in speech, Tsai Wo and Tzǔ-kung; for their administrative talents, Jan Yu and Chi Lu; for their literary acquirements, Tzǔ-yu and Tzǔ-hsia.719
