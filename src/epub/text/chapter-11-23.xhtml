XXIII771

Chi Tzǔ-jan asked whether Chung Yu and Jan Chʽiu could be called great ministers.772

The Master said, "I thought you would ask about some extraordinary individuals, and you only ask about Yu and Chʽiu!773

"What is called a great minister, is one who serves his prince according to what is right, and when he finds he cannot do so, retires.

"Now, as to Yu and Chʽiu, they may be called ordinary ministers."774

Tzǔ-jan said, "Then they will always follow their chief;—will they?"775

The Master said, "In an act of parricide or regicide, they would not follow him."
