XX981

The Master was speaking about the unprincipled course of the duke Ling of Wei, when Chʽi Kʽang said, "Since he is of such a character, how is it he does not lose his state?"982

Confucius said, "The Chung-shu Yü has the superintendence of his guests and of strangers; the litanist, Tʽo, has the management of his ancestral temple; and Wang-sun Chia has the direction of the army and forces:—with such officers as these, how should he lose his state?"983
