The Master, having come to Wu-chʽêng, heard there the sound of stringed instruments and singing.1199

Well pleased and smiling, he said, "Why use an ox knife to kill a fowl?"1200

Tzǔ-yu replied, "Formerly, Master, I heard you say,—'When the man of high station is well instructed, he loves men; when the man of low station is well instructed, he is easily ruled.'"1201

The Master said, "My disciples, Yen's words are right. What I said was only in sport."1202
