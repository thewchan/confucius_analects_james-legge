III1270

The duke Ching of Chʽi, with reference to the manner in which he should treat Confucius, said, "I cannot treat him as I would the chief of the Chi family. I will treat him in a manner between that accorded to the chief of the Chi, and that given to the chief of the Mêng family." He also said, "I am old; I cannot use his doctrines." Confucius took his departure.1271
