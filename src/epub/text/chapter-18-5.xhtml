The madman of Chʽu, Chieh-yü, passed by Confucius, singing and saying, "O Fêng! O Fêng! How is your virtue degenerated! As to the past, reproof is useless; but the future may still be provided against. Give up your vain pursuit. Give up your vain pursuit. Peril awaits those who now engage in affairs of government."1275
