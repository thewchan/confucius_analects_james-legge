XIV475

Jan Yu said, "Is our Master for the ruler of Wei?" Tzŭ-kung said, "Oh! I will ask him."476

He went in accordingly, and said, "What sort of men were Po-yi and Shu-chʽi?" "They were ancient worthies," said the Master. "Did they have any repinings because of their course?" The Master again replied, "They sought to act virtuously, and they did so; what was there for them to repine about?" On this, Tzŭ-kung went out and said, "Our Master is not for him."477
