XVIII484

The Duke of Shê asked Tzŭ-lu about Confucius, and Tzŭ-lu did not answer him.485

The Master said, "Why did you not say to him,—He is simply a man, who in his eager pursuit (of knowledge) forgets his food, who in the joy of its attainment forgets his sorrows, and who does not perceive that old age is coming on?"486
