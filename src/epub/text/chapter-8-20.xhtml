Shun had five ministers, and the empire was well-governed.570

King Wu said, "I have ten able ministers."561

Confucius said, "Is not the saying that talents are difficult to find, true? Only when the dynasties of Tʽang and Yu met, were they more abundant than in this of Chou, yet there was a woman among them. The able ministers were no more than nine men.562

"King Wên possessed two of the three parts of the empire, and with those he served the dynasty of Yin. The virtue of the house of Chou may be said to have reached the highest point indeed."563
