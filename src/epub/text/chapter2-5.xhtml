Chapter V113

Mêng Yi114 asked what filial piety was. The Master said, "It is not being disobedient."

Soon after, as Fan Chʽih115 was driving him, the Master told him, saying, "Mêng-sun asked me what filial piety was, and I answered him,—'not being disobedient.'"

Fan Chʽih said, "What did you mean?" The Master replied, "That parents, when alive, be served according to propriety; that, when dead, they should be buried according to propriety; and that they should be sacrificed to according to propriety."
