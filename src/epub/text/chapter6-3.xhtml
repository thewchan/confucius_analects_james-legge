Chapter III382

Tzŭ-hua being employed on a mission to Chʽi,383 the disciple Jan requested grain for his mother.384 The Master said, "Give her a fu."385 Yen requested more. "Give her an yü," said the Master. Yen gave her five ping.386

The Master said, "When Chʽih was proceeding to Chʽi, he had fat horses to his carriage, and wore light furs. I have heard that a superior man helps the distressed, but does not add to the wealth of the rich."387

Yüan Ssŭ being made governor of his town by the Master, he gave him nine hundred measures of grain, but Ssŭ declined them.388

The Master said, "Do not decline them. May you not give them away in the neighborhoods, hamlets, towns, and villages?"389
